Living on Another Planet

        Featherap is a imaginary world on another planet, where humankind settle after leaving earth due to severe pollution and other environmental problems. People begin their live on this planet by excavating huge rocks, turning them into caves. Besides space to fulfill the basic living need, humankind decide to build a museum, hoping their children understand the culture human once have on earth.
