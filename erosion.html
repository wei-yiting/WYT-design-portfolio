Erosion

Material and Spatial Experiment

2014

        This work is an material experiment aiming on behavior of erosion by using acetone to erode styrofoam. The goal is to create void from a solid cube of styrofoam and design the spatial arrangement through applying acetone at specific area with the control of certain amount of acetone. The creation of space in this project is more like carving rather than typical constructing.
