Umbrellight

Installation

2012

Umbrella, Water and Light Bulb

        UmbrelLight is an installation work at a windy, dark, semi-outdoor space. The concept is to use an upside-down umbrella to contain a thin layer of water, with light bulb on top of it. As the wind blowing through the water, ripples combine with the light create dynamic light effect underneath as if the light comes alive. Also, to create a new space experience through installation, a great amount of umbrelLights are staggered within the place to provide a new form of ceiling.
