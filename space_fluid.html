Space Fluid

Sculpture

2014

        This work of sculpture tries to capture moment of fluid into spatial expression. By mixing liquid plaster and wax together and then melt out the wax after plaster is set, the moment which two fluid flow among each other is captured into a solid state. The volume once occupied by fluid wax become the void. Space fluid bring the dynamic motion of fluid into a solid representation.
