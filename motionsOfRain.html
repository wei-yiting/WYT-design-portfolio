Motions of Rain

Experimental Drawings

2013

Drip, Flow, Whirl

        This sequence of drawings tries to capture several motions of rains. Unlike traditional way of drawing, the interaction and relationship between the ink and the canvas is the main focus, which represent the surface and rain water. The attempt is to reproduce moments of dynamic scenes such as splashing, flowing and whirling onto the canvas.
