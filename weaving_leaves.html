Weaving Leaves

Industrial Design

2011

        Weaving Leaves is a design of light aiming to reproduce the scene of sunshine below trees, as sunlight is mostly blocked by layers of leaves, with only little light pass through layers of leaves. The surface pattern of this light is designed to capture form of leaves which seem to weave among each other. Through the composition of mutiple layers stacking outwards light bulb, the sense of sunshine below the tree is created.
